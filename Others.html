1What to pack in the suitcase?

If you travel to London, it is essential to bring some coat, even if you have planned your trip in summer, because at night it usually cools a lot. In addition, you should not forget an umbrella or a raincoat, since rains are quite common in this destination. We also recommend you bring a first aid kit (remember that in some countries to buy the medicines sold here without a prescription, they can only be purchased with a prescription). For example, Ibuprofen pills are much smaller than those found in Spain. Considering these basic notions, let's move on to preparing the luggage.

Covid-19

Regardless of whether you are vaccinated for Covid-19, as outlined by the Department for Transport and the Department of Health and Social Care of the United Kingdom, to travel to England it is not necessary to book and pay for a Covid-19 test. You also won't have to fill out a passenger locator form and you won't be quarantined when you arrive in the country.

Assistance

Although to travel to this nation it is not mandatory to have a travel assistance service, it is best to hire a travel assistance plan for London and the rest of the cities in England.
